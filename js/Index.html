Happy

We understand that you as a parent would not like to see your child happy when they are grounded. This is why we have created this software to make sure that if your child is smiling or laughing during their period of grounding we will automatically close all the open tabs so that the feeling of sadness will always remain inside of them until they are ungrounded.

Fear

We the people here at Enhanced Parent Parental Controls know how important grounding is to make a child's future bright. This is why we have decided to take the suffering of your child to the next level. When the child shows fear on his or her face. This extension will automatically clone the current tab multiple times.

The suffering of your child is our top priority
